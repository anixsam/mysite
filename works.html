1.Bike Anti Theft System (B-A-T)

B-A-T System is a device designed with the collaboration of my team, members of the team including, Alby Kavanal, Alan M Eldho, Afnitha Sahier. B-A-T SYSTEM is an anti-theft and security device for the bike when it is parked.Our goal with this project is to provide maximum protection for the bike when it is parked and also if it is snatched.We can remotely kill the bikeâ€™s engine if the bike is stolen.

This Device connects to the main wiring harness of the bike.The system have a separate harness so that connection makes easy and no cutting of wires required.This device is connected to the user by the help of an android application

Installation of the device

This Device is designed to connect and operate seamlessly to our bike.The operation of the device is simple as the UI of the app is simple.User can interact with the bike using a smartphone which contains the feature of locking and unlocking the bike, also there is a feature where we can detect whether anybody turn the ignition of the bike also the owner can remotely turn off the bike in case of necessary. It comes with an additional feature of Find My Bike where owner can find the bike from the parking lot. We can also detect the gps location of tht bike realtime on the app. There is also option to review the account details of the owner.
